A spoiler-free guide to the emotional world of the series.

Synopsis

In Your Radiant Season follows Chan, a bright animator whose life seems to move with the warmth of summer, and Haran, a fashion designer who has sealed herself inside a quiet emotional winter.

The emotional premise

The site reads the series through seasons: summer as motion, winter as survival, and spring as the possibility of healing.
